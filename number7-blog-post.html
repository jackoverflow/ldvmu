#7 – What Can the DaVinci Mind(s) Do for Me? My Children? And the World?

DaVinci Minds built the modern world. All the technological progress you see about you and use and rely on each and every day are the fruits of an unseen and unsung DaVinci Minds. That most of them are unknown and not rich or famous because of their efforts is not the point of their existence. Without these DaVinci Minds in action and bouts of higher DaVinci Mind consciousness we would be living in cave trying to make fire.

And we would also still be acting as animals and brutes. DaVinci-ism and DaVinci Minds built civilization and gave us the thoughts, ideas, thinkings, and principles which challenged and improved and stretched our minds into a semblance of civilization from brutal animalism.

And we have a long way to go yet. A lot of challenges to overcome… A lot of ways to grow more… Higher and higher DaVinci Minds consciousness and achievements to attain…

And the safety and security and happiness which all of us crave and work towards.

What can the DaVinci Minds do for you?

A great many things.

First, we can help you recognize and realize who you are. What you really are. And what you can be.

Second, we can help guide you in learning how best to utilize your talents and improve the tendrils of DaVinci Mind ability with you. And within everyone.

Third, we’re building a community for DaVinci Minds. For fellowship, for resources, for learning, for projects. Join Us! And help yourself to the many benefits of being a DaVinci and building a community of DaVinci Minds.

And your children. Children are our future. Our only True Future. And we often treat them so poorly. They are all little budding DaVinci Minds trying to flower into Full DaVinci Minds. With fulfillment and happiness…So let’s help them!

And as a result, the world will be a better place.

And we will be better people, individuals, and Souls.

We made it out of the caves with the blasts of DaVinci Minds.

Now, it’s time to flower DaVinci Minds through the world so as to attain the wondrous future that is waiting for us all!
